Questions for either parents or students

What is the educational content within FinBizify?

FinBizify is intended to cover learning content of most commmon Business and Personal Finance courses. FinBizify is intended for a student to learn business, marketing, financial, and strategy concepts and is not a course offered by a public, private, or charter high school.

Do schools need to opt in?

No. FinBizify is offered directly to students. Teenagers and parents can access without requiring public or private school adoption.
